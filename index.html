About

Super Bazooka Boy is a fast-paced action-platformer that puts you in control of a hero armed with an explosive bazooka. Developed by thesitestay.com, this game features thrilling physics-based destruction, strategic shooting, and challenging levels. With intuitive controls and exciting gameplay, Super Bazooka Boy offers an unforgettable arcade experience!
